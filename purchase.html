Purchase Policy

Acceptable Payment Methods

We accept cash, all major credit cards, and PayPalTM for payment on puppies. Any service charge associated with credit card or Paypal purchases will be added to the purchase price of the puppy. Credit cards are only accepted in person and will not be accepted over the phone or email. You may use a credit card through PayPalTM if you are not paying in person.

PayPalTM Service Fees

The fee of 2.9% plus $ 0.30 is added to the purchase price of any puppy.

PayPal HereTM Service Fees

The fee of 2.7% is added to the purchase price of any puppy.

Shipping Costs

We will not ship puppies. If you are within four (4) hours of Portland Metro area, we are able to deliver your new puppy for an additional cost. The cost is figured on a per mile basis at $1 per mile (calculated on one way mileage). Payment of the shipping cost is required prior to travel. Shipping costs are non-refundable.

Placing a Hold on Puppies

We will hold puppies for 24 hours without a deposit. A $200 hold deposit is required on all holds of greater than 24 hours and are not refundable. Hold monies are subject to the service charge if paid through Paypal or major credit card. Any hold deposit will be applied as payment to the purchase price of a puppy.

Private Sales Only

All puppies are sold to personal homes only. We will not sell puppies to any retailers, brokers, associations, clubs or other entity.

Full AKC Registration

All our puppies are sold with the ability to obtain full registration through the AKC.
