The Litter Box Meet the Puppies

Information

The litter was born on March 10th. The soonest they will be ready for their new homes is May 5th. Pictures of each puppy and info on each will be updated in the next few days.

A few things to know about about this litter:

  • The litter has already been registered with the AKC.
  • All puppies are AKC registerable by their new owners.
  • Dew claws were removed on March 14th.
  • All puppies will be dewormed and have vaccinations at 5 weeks.
  • May 5th will be the soonest they are ready for their new home.
  • All puppies have a Health Warranty.
  • All new owners are required to sign our Rehoming Agreement.
  • At the time of purchase, you will be provided with a three generation family tree for the puppy and all necessary paperwork to register your new puppy with the AKC.
  • Raising a Great Dane is a big responsibility and each new home is screened before placement.
